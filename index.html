HTML creates the look of the text and other parts of web pages through the use of elements which are created with tags. Tags can be closed or not, this means that the Text or URL reference or what have you is surrounded by a greater than sign a letter or group of letters denoting the type of element then a less than sign and then the text and then the close to the element which consists of a greater than sign, a slash and the same letter or group of letters and another less than sign.

Elements come in two flavors either inline or Block, the difference being the Block elements. encase the text or what have you in a box, while the inline element does not.
